Army set

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Collection of clothing items related to the military. Mainly items a grunt or specop themed character could wear.

Most of the items listed here can be found in mil. surplus stores, clothing stores, pawn shops, military outposts, military bunkers or worn by zombie soldiers and dead soldiers found randomly around the world.
