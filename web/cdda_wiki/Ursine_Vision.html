Ursine Vision URSINE_EYE URSINE
Cancels: Fey Eyes, Reptilian Eyes, Avian Eyes, Feline Eyes, Compound Eyes, Night Vision, High Night Vision, Full Night Vision, Near-Sighted
Point cost: 1
"Your visual processing has shifted: though you can see better in the dark, you're nearsighted in the light. Maybe glasses would help. Activate to toggle NV-visible areas on or off."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • Can be activated, use the [ mutation menu. Starts active.
  • Near-Sightedness in the light can be counteracted with contacts or eyeglasses.
  • +4.5 squares to sight radius in darkness.
