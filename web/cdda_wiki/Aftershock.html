Aftershock

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Aftershock is a mod included in the base game. It includes additional sci-fi/cyberpunk content. In 0.F (Frank), it is being turned into a total-conversion mod; see the Aftershock README's design document section.

A partial sampling of some of its content is below.

Bionics

  • Solar Panels: Charges bionic power from natural light. Operates at only 3.7% normal rate underground.
  • Wind Turbine: Charges bionic power from wind.
  • Deployable Grenade Launcher: Can fire grenades from wrist.
  • Linguistic Coprocessor: Speed up reading speed by 15%.
  • Dopamine Stimulators: Provide a morale boost of 20.
  • Counteractive Reflexes: Provide an extra move whenever character is hit in melee.
  • Melee Optimization Unit: Provides speed bonus after melee attack.
  • Neurosoft Aeronautics: Provides knowledge of how to fly a helicopter.
  • Cranial Bomb: Harmful bionic that can kill character upon timed detonation.
  • Skullgun: Head-mounted single-shot pistol.
