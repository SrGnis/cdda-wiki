“ Antianxiety agent with a powerful sedative effect. May cause dissociation and loss of memory. It is dangerously addictive, and withdrawal from regular use should be gradual. ”

It will temporarily cancel the effects of anxiety, like the Hoarder trait.

Taking Xanax gives the Xanax disease for 900 turns.
