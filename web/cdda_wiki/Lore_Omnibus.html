List of Instances

XEDRA Personnel

  • Admin
  • Amad, Davids, Vaughn, and Kohler - A quartet of individuals associated with XEDRA, each of which is referred to by last name only. It is likely they are engineers of some kind.
  • Dr. Barnhoff
  • Dr. Bianchi
  • Dr. Dionne
  • Dr. Garcia
  • Dr. Heisenstein
  • Dr. Hofstadter
  • Dr. Holstein
  • Dr. Isha
  • Dr. Maiar - Dr. Maiar seems to be eccentrically amoral, even by XEDRA standards. It is implied that XEDRA forces him to stop his research and become subject to involuntary experimentation.
  • Dr. Sattler
  • Dr. Savage - XEDRA researcher with a doctorate in political science
  • Dr. Sidhu
  • Dr. Silverstein
  • Dr. Takatoshi - a senior XEDRA researcher
  • Dr. Thoreaux
  • Dr. Versed
  • Hendelson - is likely a doctoral peer within XEDRA, despite never being referenced with the Dr. honorific
  • Siobhan - is likely a doctoral peer within XEDRA, she is mentioned in connection with Resin research
  • Herrera - presumably an individual associated with XEDRA that has an electronics/mechanical area of responsibility
  • Mandrake - a security officer who reanimates after death and becomes a test subject
  • Melchior - an AI

US Military

  • Ariel_Dabrowski
  • Andrea Sylver
  • General Alex Findlay
  • General Baker - a general who commits suicide rather than launch nuclear weapons against American civilians
  • General Carlsberg
  • Shawn Singer - NSA spokesperson

Other

  • Dr. Alice Feyn
  • Alex_Ackerwith - Governor of Rhode Island
  • Brett Xiang - missing person
  • Constantine_Dvorak - Undersecretary of Nuclear Security
  • Robert Shane
  • Danny Veil - spokesperson for Uncanny
  • Dr. Andrew Morton - Harvard epidemiologist
  • Ellen Grimes - a director of the CDC
  • Elton Moosek - an entrepreneur known primarily for his high opinion of himself
  • Emperor Tsang Gu Yen - a rumored invader of Vermont
  • Linda Garrison - FEMA spokesperson
  • Linda Iridani - DARPA spokesperson with knowledge and/or responsibility within the orbit of Bionics research
  • President Oswald - President of the US, successor to Pres. Toffer
  • President Toffer - President of the US, predecessor to Pres. Oswald, known for eliminating consumer nuclear protections
  • Qiang Ye
  • Randall Balakrishnan - Deputy Secretary of Energy
  • Subject CC-09 and Subject CC-11 - a pair of Mi-go in the custody of XEDRA
  • Subject T3D - XEDRA test subject
  • Subject TP92 - XEDRA test subject
  • Simon Bellevue - rat attack fatality, associated with "Lab 24"
