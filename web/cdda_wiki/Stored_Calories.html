Stored calories is one of the hidden stats tracker for the calories stored by your character's organism.

What is it for?

  • It's a value that keeps track of how many calories your body accumulated due to eating food and drinking caloric beverages.
  • Whenever you digest food or drinks their nutrition is converted to body calories and stored in this value.
  • On more down to earth terms this value tells you how fat or skinny you are.
  • Stored calories are used to determine your hunger level. If you have low stored calories, you are malnourished and you feel more hunger.

What's the normal level?

  • Normal, healthy level of stored calories is 55,000 kcal.
  • Getting below that will push you towards malnutrition.
  • Getting above that will push you towards obesity.
  • Both extremes bring their own penalties, and finally death.

Tiers

There are tiers of stored calories, and there is a bit of an insight on them in the character status menu (@).

Morbidly Obese "You have far more fat than is healthy or useful. It is causing you major problems."
Very Obese "You have too much fat. It impacts your day to day health and wellness."
Obese "You've definitely put on a lot of extra weight. Although it's helpful in times of famine, this is too much and is impacting your health."
Overweight "You've put on some extra pounds. Nothing too excessive but it's starting to impact your health and waistline a bit."
Normal "You look to be a pretty healthy weight, with some fat to last you through the winter but nothing excessive."
Underweight "You are thin, thinner than is healthy. You are less resilient to going without food."
Emaciated "You are very unhealthily underweight, nearing starvation."
Skeletal "You have very little meat left on your bones. You appear to be starving."

Cheating

If you want to know how much food you have stored in your stomach, know your body mass index and other stats related the new weight and food system, press your debug menu key, select info and then (g)select game state. These stomach and weight info will be in your message log after some other game state related popups.
