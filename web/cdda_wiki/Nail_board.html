Notes

Has a decent amount of attack damage for an early game item, is also extremely handy against electric enemies such as shocker zombies, as one can attack them without being shocked, even though it has metal nails in it.

However, it can be a very fragile weapon, and is likely to break easily. Luckily, this isn't a major problem because nails and planks are common items from disassembling furniture.

Also despite the presence of metal nails, it cannot be pulled away by zombie technicians.
