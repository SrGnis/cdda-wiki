  • A green material name means that the item can be repaired, fitted (if appropriate) or reinforced with a sewing kit or bone needle (using the tailoring skill).
  • An orange material name means that the item can be repaired, fitted (if appropriate) or reinforced with soldering iron (using the mechanics skill).

Head encumbrance has no penalties; it simply limits how much you can put on. Characters cannot wear more headgear if their current encumbrance is higher than zero. This means that items without encumbrance can be worn under another, more cumbersome piece of headgear.

Many pieces of headgear also cover the eyes and mouth- this means both protection and encumbrance apply to these as well.

Headgear environmental protection protects the eyes too.
