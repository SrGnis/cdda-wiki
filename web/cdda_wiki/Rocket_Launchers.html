Only use these firearms when a big boss monster absolutely needs to be annihilated in one shot and when you are skilled enough in launchers, these are the most powerful firearms in all of the game, and have the rarest ammo of them all. If you aren't skilled enough with launchers, you could waste a good rocket, or worse, your life, reduced to a mass of gibs on the ground.
