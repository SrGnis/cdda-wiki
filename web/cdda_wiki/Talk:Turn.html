I've updated the internal links to the preferred method. As you can see, when using internal links (links to other pages of this wiki) it's simpler if you just put the name of the item, etc in double brackets. You can change the displayed text of the link by adding a | after the link, but still inside the double brackets.

Examples

Internal link to the Vehicles page: 

[[Vehicles]] is displayed as Vehicles

Internal link changing the displayed link text: 

[[Vehicles#Displays|Vehicle display area]] is displayed as Vehicle display area
