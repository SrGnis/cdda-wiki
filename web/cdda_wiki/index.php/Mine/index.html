M mine
See Cost 2


Underground location, which you enter via the Mine shaft, the mine is a long corridor that usually goes down several more levels that eventually will have a special (and usually dangerous) ending.

It may have as finale:

  • Pedestal with petrified eye.
  • Meeting with the Thing.
  • Spiral cavern. (these always spawn below the mine ending, but are not always accessible, you can dig down to reach them)
  • Amigara fault.

If you don't mind being fully spoiled you may want to read "Guide: Mine".
