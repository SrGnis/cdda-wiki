Remember, it is not 'dying' it is 'learning how to do better next time'!

Not just humans were brought back as undead abominations during the Cataclysm. All sorts of animals, from man's best friend to the majestic black bear and even the moose, have risen from the dead.

These monsters seem to become more and more common as the days pass and seasons change, somewhat replacing the living ones.
