Necropolis

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Necropolis is a type of city that can be found rarely instead of a regular city. A Necropolis can be identified by its more organized layout, when compared to normal cities. It should be noted that these towns have a number of radioactive craters in it, so care should be taken.

Besides normal zombies, one can also find Irradiated wanderers here.

The town contains:

  • A Police station
  • 4 Houses
  • A Pump station
  • A Gas station with an attached Garage
  • A Grocery store with an attached garage containing a lift
  • An Abandoned storefront
  • A Salon
  • A Bank
  • A Furniture store
  • Several Parking Lots
  • A Church
  • A Pawn shop
  • A Park
  • A Clothing Store
  • A Bar
  • A Gardening Store
  • A Fast food restaurant
  • A Restaurant

Of note is that the Necropolis will always contain these buildings, with the layout also always remaining the same. The only change may be its rotation.

Also, one can encounter a Vault beneath the sewers of the Necropolis, it is good to bring a good supply of lockpicks in order to get there as there are iron bar doors in the tunnels, but beware, unlike the usual sparsely populated sewers, the ones in the Necropolis are infested with irradiated wanderers.
