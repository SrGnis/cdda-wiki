  • A green material name means that the item can be repaired, fitted (if appropriate) or reinforced with a sewing kit or bone needle (using the tailoring skill).
  • An orange material name means that the item can be repaired, fitted (if appropriate) or reinforced with soldering iron (using the mechanics skill).

Per one torso encumbrance level melee and dodge skills are decreased by 1 and swimming and melee attack points costs are increased by 80 and 20 points, respectively.

Apart from rain coats, any torso piece with warmth of 35 or more will protect you from getting wet.
