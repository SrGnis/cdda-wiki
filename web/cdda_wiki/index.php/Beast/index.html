Beast

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Beast THRESH_BEAST Targeted Mutation Only Not purifiable
Point cost: 1
"It's about time you grew out. Now that you've matured, it is time to make something of yourself."

The opposite of Bird. Amazing in close combat, but extremely high-maintenance.

The threshold mutation of this category is called Beast ingame.
