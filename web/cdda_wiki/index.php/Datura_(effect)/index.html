Datura (effect)

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Datura is a status effect applied by consumption of datura seeds, containing a potent psychoactive substance (Anticholinergic Tropane Alkaloids).

"Buy the ticket, take the ride. The datura has you now."

  • The datura effect is applied when you consume datura seeds.
  • Their consumption by characters with Spiritual trait adds to morale - trip to nirvana, huh?
  • The datura effect from a single dose can last randomly between 3 and 13 hours.
  • You can lethally overdose from eating 3 seeds if you're unlucky, but due to randomness of added duration it's more probable after around 5+ consecutive doses.
  • The datura effect stops asthma attacks. It can be used as a last resort medicine if you don't have an inhaler on you.
  • The datura effect applies penalties to your stats:
    • -6 perception
    • -3 dexterity
  • The datura effect has a chance to increase your thirst up to 20 max, one in 8 chance per tick.
  • Depending on its concentration in the body, it can trigger any of the following effects (listed from least to most severe; each can trigger independently):
    • moderate chance of focus reduction when duration > 100 minutes
    • increase in stimulation when stim < 20 and duration > 200 minutes
    • high chance of focus reduction when duration > 300 minutes, cumulative with the first effect's reduction
    • moderate pain reduction when duration > 400 minutes
    • long lasting hallucinations when duration > 500 minutes
    • strong pain reduction when duration > 600 minutes
      • This is with an extra chance of nausea and being downed when duration > 800 minutes. "You're experiencing loss of basic motor skills and blurred vision. Your mind recoils in horror, unable to communicate with your spinal column." "You stagger and fall!"
    • definite focus reduction when duration > 700 minutes
    • severe hallucinations, potent pain reduction when duration > 800 minutes
    • severe hallucinations, moderate chance of inflicting pain & nausea when duration > 1200 minutes
    • severe hallucinations, high chance of inflicting pain & nausea, overdose warning ("The possibility of physical and mental collapse is now very real.") when duration > 1400 minutes.
    • chance of death from overdose by heart attack ("Your heart spasms painfully and stops, dragging you back to reality as you die.") when duration > 1800 minutes.
  • The datura effect makes you light sensitive. Direct sunlight causes irritation, pain and loss of focus. It will also wake you up from sleep.
  • The datura effect can be removed by the Blood filter bionic.
