Arachnid THRESH_SPIDER Targeted Mutation Only Not purifiable
Point cost: 1
"Well, maybe you'll just have to make your own world wide web."

Spiders work well for a kiting melee style. Retreat with Fleet-Footed, trap your pursuers with Web Weaver, and dispatch them with Folding Fangs and Strongly Venomous. Full Night Vision and Infrared Vision put you in charge of the nighttime battlefield.

The threshold mutation of this category is called Arachnid ingame.

Notes

You need to make a nest with "thick webs" (the kind that blocks line-of-sight) to be able to sleep. Activate Web Weaver, and then pause on the same tile at least three turns to make cobwebs -> webs -> thick webs.

With Furred Plate you can walk stark naked and recieve a full +9 to your dodge skill! But if you are in winter its probably a bad idea...
