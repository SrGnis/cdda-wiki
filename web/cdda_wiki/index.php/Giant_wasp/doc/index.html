Giant wasp/doc

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
  • Giant wasps can found singularly in fields, forests, and in large numbers in and around wasp nests (see below).
  • Giant Wasp nests spawn inside of houses, and are recorded as regular houses on the map. The house has the same loot table and contents as a regular house, however the player may have to 's'mash the paper walls out of the way to get to them.
  • Giant wasps can provide chitin when butchered, which is an essential component in the fabrication of some types of armor and in plant fertilizers.
  • These mutant giant insects are rather dangerous to an underprepared and unskilled player, as they can attack quickly and multiple times during a turn, their venom can weaken the player, making it even more difficult to fight them, and they have decent dodge against players, avoiding them until better skilled and equipped is recommended.
  • Almost always try to stay out of sight of them. As they have a movement rate of 150 and are quite aggressive. You are best prepared to challenges if you can kill one isolated exemplar with 3-4 high damage strikes in melee.
  • For more information, check the Dealing with Wasps guide.
