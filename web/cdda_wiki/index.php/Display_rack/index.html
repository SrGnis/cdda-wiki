A set of metal shelves often used in stores to displaying wares. Applying a hacksaw to it will destroy it, yielding 1-3 pipes and 1 chunk of steel.

Construction

Made From:

  • Tool: wrench, (hammer | stone hammer | hatchet)
  • Ingredients: 12x pipe

Smashes Into:

  • Ingredients: 2-7 scrap metal, 0-3 chunk of steel, 0-3 pipe

Notes:

Holds 7000 volume, compared to the 4000 a regular ground tile holds. Very common in shops and large kitchens. Cannot be moved through yet do not block line of sight. Can't be dragged. Their contents can be seen from a distance, unlike cupboards.
