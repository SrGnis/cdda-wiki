There are four seasons in C:DDA, which have various effects on gameplay, such as ambient temperature, the weather, and what kind of things you can get from foraging and what kind of plants grow fruit.

Spring

Spring is the first (in most scenarios), and one of the two safer seasons in Cataclysm Dark Days Ahead, alongside autumn.

Spring is followed by summer, since summer is deadly for the unprepared, players should spend their first spring preparing for the harsh heat that is to come.

For more information see the page on spring.

Summer

Summer is the second (first if playing The Next Summer scenario) and one of the two deadlier seasons in Cataclysm Dark Days Ahead, alongside winter.

Summer is followed by autumn, which is much more welcoming, giving players a breather before winter.

For more information see the page on summer.

Autumn

For more information see the page on autumn.

Winter

Winter is the fourth (first in the Ambush and Sheltered scenarios) and one of the two deadlier seasons in Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead, alongside summer.

Winter is followed by spring, a more warm and welcoming season.

For more information see the page on winter.
