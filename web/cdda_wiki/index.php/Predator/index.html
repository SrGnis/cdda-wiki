Predator PRED3 Targeted Mutation Only Not purifiable Threshold Mutation LIZARD BEAST FELINE LUPINE URSINE SPIDER CHIMERA RAPTOR
Requires: Carnivore, Bear
Additional requirement: Hunter
Cancels: Pacifist
Changes to: Apex Predator
Leads to: Sapiovore
Point cost: 3
"You consider yourself something other than human and no longer empathize with them. Combat skills are easy to learn and maintain, but your critical thinking, so characteristic to these creatures, suffers."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • Modifies intelligence by -1.
  • Increases chance of intimidating NPCs by 4%.
  • Doubles the amount of experience combat skills get when used. Removes guilt from killing.
  • Bonuses to learning combat skills.
  • Has a 50% chance to prevent skill rust in combat skills.
  • If it prevents skill rust, it has a one in 15600 chance of remembering your old hunts. Your heart races as you recall your most recent hunt.. Which slightly increases Stim level.
