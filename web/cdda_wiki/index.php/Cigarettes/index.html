“ A food item ”



“ A mixture of dried tobacco leaf, pesticides and chemical additives, rolled into a filtered paper tube. Stimulates mental acuity and reduces appetite. Highly addictive and hazardous to health. ”

These will boost your dexterity, intelligence, and perception for a short time. They are quite addictive.

Cigarettes give the nicotine status effect for 200 turns when taken.
