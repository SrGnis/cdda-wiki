Greens, like NourishMart and Foodplace, is a grocery chain.

The CDDA brand ecosystem contains at least three grocery store chains. While you may have heard of Foodplace, it has at least two competitors with significant marketshare: Greens and NourishMart. So next time you're starving, get out there and explore the world of grocery.
