Not all species coming from the netherum are as successful or widespread as the blob. There are many "lesser" Nether creatures who are not as expansionistic or have found Earth to be a hostile environment; make no mistake, however - they are anything but harmless.

Coming in all shapes and sizes (many of which didn't exist in this dimension), they are powerful, varied, and unpredictable. Many have powers and abilities unlike anything humanity has ever witnessed - or, in some cases, even considered might be real.

It is believed that the nethereum, the netherum, and the nether all reference the same concept, and can be used interchangeably. That said, it is likely that "netherum" is simply a common misspelling of "nethereum."
