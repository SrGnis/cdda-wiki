Trap tr_dissector

Automated high tech dissector. Uses state of the art high powered lasers to cut up bodies for analysis.

Don't step in it. Will randomly hurt all player's body parts up to 10 (to hands an feet), 12 (arms & legs), 15 (head) and 20 points (torso).

Does not work on robots (does give a 4 noise error message). Will deal 60 damage to non robotic monsters (monsters not of the species ROBOT).

It creates 10 noise when activated.

Cannot be removed normally. Starts hidden.

Cannot be made by the player. Spawns in labs.
