Shark Teeth SHARKTEETH Not purifiable Threshold Mutation FISH
Requires: Fangs
Cancels: Fangs, Incisors, Saber Teeth, Mandibles, Folding Fangs, Proboscis, Beak, Woodpecker Beak, Hummingbird Beak,
Point cost: 3 / Visibility: 10 / Ugliness: 8
"Your teeth have grown incredibly sharp and are formed of very dense calcium material. In addition to making you eat much faster, you can use them as a lethal natural weapon, as long as your anatomy favors it. They also grow very fast, and you harmlessly shed them more or less at random."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • Unless you have the Lupine Muzzle, Reptilian Muzzle, Rodent Muzzle mutations, this mutation gives an additional attack, with a at minimum one in 20 chance of activating, using the mouth body part, doing 25 points of stab damage.
  • Increases chance of intimidating NPCs by 5%.
( Edit Notes )
