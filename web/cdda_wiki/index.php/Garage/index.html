O garage
See Cost 5
Extras building

Here you'll find tools and parts needed for building vehicles or repairing and installing vehicle parts. You also can occasionally find either complete vehicles or chassis here. You may also find a winch which can be used to open or close the garage door, creating some noise in the process.
