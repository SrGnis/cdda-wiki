Picket fence

Jump to navigation Jump to search
| picket fence
Movement 150
Action none
Flags bashable transparent diggable flammable2 noitem thin_obstacle

A neat wooden fence, often found in the yards of houses. If a crowbar is applied, it will be destroyed, yielding 3 two by fours and 6 nails.

Construction

Smashes Into:

  • Ingredients: 1-3 two by four, 2-6 nails, 1 splintered wood
