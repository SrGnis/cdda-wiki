Thanks for your help on scenarios and other things; one pointer re "obsolete"

Hi, and thanks for all your help! (Due to development work, what I have locally is the latest experimental, so feedback on things from before that is especially helpful!) One thing on scenarios (and some other places one might use Template:Obsolete): Please put <noinclude> and </noinclude> around the obsolete template, as otherwise when the page gets included somewhere - such as on the scenarios page - the obsolete notice gets pasted on that page.
