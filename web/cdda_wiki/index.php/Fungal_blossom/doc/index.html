Fungal blossom/doc

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Found in the fungal flowers area. Like fungaloids, these are passive and constantly emit spores around them; unlike fungaloids, they are immobile.

While in the reality bubble, it will emit a haze every 25 turns that turns the surrounding area within a 3-tile radius into fungal terrain. Similar to fungaloids but much faster. This haze causes spore infection without proper protection.

Trees caught in the haze can be turned into additional fungal blossoms. This can lead to very fast butterfly effect.
