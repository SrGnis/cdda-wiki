“ A sturdy, heavy stick. Makes a decent melee weapon. ”


Notes

  • Heavy sticks can be found commonly in forests. They can also be obtained by activating or (() deconstructing a long stick (if you have a cutting tool). You can get long sticks by smashing young trees, or by smashing or (() deconstructing certain windows.
  • In the past sticks could be sawed into planks. But that has been removed, you can make two by fours out of logs now.
