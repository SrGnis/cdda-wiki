Grocery store

^>v< grocery store
See Cost 5
Extras building

Grocery stores primarily stock food and drinks, especially raw fruits and vegetables in the produce section. They're also excellent sources of pasta, glass-jarred sauces, and other nonperishable food. Cooking equipment & other useful tools can be had in the hardware aisle, and some magazines and snack foods in the checkout racks. Finally, grocery stores may have shopping carts which greatly expands your inventory space.

Their broad selection, semi-perishable stock, and generally little-to-no zeds inside make these an excellent target on the first night. Even in the later seasons, if you find one you should plan a thorough looting run. They're worth it.

Lore

There are currently 4 grocery store chains that exist in the CDDA universe:

  • Foodplace
  • Greens
  • Marigold Market
  • NourishMart
