“ A warm covering that protects the head and face from cold. ”

This is a very light clothing item made of cotton, which covers 95% of your head and mouth, and which has no storage capacity. It doesn't occupy too much space, doesn't encumber you at all, and will provide some warmth to the areas it covers.

It offers very low protection against bashing attacks and very low protection against cutting ones (that includes bullets), while offering no protection from environmental hazards.

It has no extra particularities.

This clothing item can be repaired, fitted (if appropriate) and reinforced via the tailoring skill, by using a sewing kit or bone needle and rags.
