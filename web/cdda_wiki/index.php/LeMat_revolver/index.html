A Pietta reproduction of the civil war era LeMat revolver, a rare and unusual cap & ball .44-caliber revolver. While its original non-standard .42 or .35 caliber curbed its usefulness for the CSA army, this reproduction is offered in more prevalent .44 caliber. Despite modern quality materials, the design is still rather delicate.
