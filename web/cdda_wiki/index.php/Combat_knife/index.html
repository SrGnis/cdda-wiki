“ Designed for combat, and deadly in the right hands. Can be used to butcher corpses. ”



Notes

Quick to swing, has good butchering for what it is, and does plenty of cut damage. Very good for cut training or an early weapon. If you start as a soldier or soldier-like profession, you may start with this.
