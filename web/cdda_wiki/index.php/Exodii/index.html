“ Oh HO! Well, well. Now you're yarkin' the King's Anglic. Aye, us'n can fix ye good, if'n ye're tassed to repay it in kind. Us'll speak it as clear-like as this Rubik can. If ye'n use it to put the dead back in the ground, us'n can fix ye with some goods like this, aye. Stuck in the flesh and to the bone, wired into the brain. Ye kennit? If no, us'll speak to the Great Grey for a pint o' the ol' clarity-draught.  ”

Exodii are non-hostile extradimensional cyborg humans who scavenge Earth for oil and brains. In the current Experimental, they are primary source of Bionics.

Lore

Main article: Lore#The_Exodii

Finding the Exodii

If you'd like to find this faction in your current world, you have three options. You can hunt around for one of their sub-bases, the unusual stone barn, and read the sign there to be directed to the Rocky Butte, or you can complete the first quest for either Smokes or the Old Guard Representative in the Refugee Center. After that, talk to the old guard rep, or the trucker in the back bay (near where you fought the zombies for Smokes) and you can get a quest that will lead you to the Rocky Butte.

Bases

Unusual stone barn

^ Unusual stone barn
See Cost 2


Warehouse containing metal scraps and arcane furniture. There is a sign in front of the entrance; examining it will pinpoint the location of the nearest Rocky Butte on overmap.

Rocky Butte

? ground-level entrance
See Cost 4


Main Exodii base in New England. 4 z-levels tall castle teeming with cyborgs, loot and arcane furniture.

Rubik

Anglic-speaking Exodii merchant residing on the second floor of Rocky Butte. Sells medieval armors, Romanian rifles, armor templates, Bionics and bionic accessories. Can arrange a safe installation or removal of bionics for $20 credit

Once survivor brings in 1L of anesthetic, Rubik will install cable charger and storage module for free, and will be able to arrange relatively safe surgeries in the future.

Buying high tier CBMs requires faction trust. Currently it can be acquired by some dialogue options, completing quests and regularly visiting Rubik.

Benzete

Exodii navigator. Currently doesn't exist in the game, is mentioned by Rubik in dialogue.

Exodii crash site

A small junk heap found in fields. It is a home for multiple hostile zomborgs, a moderately dangerous enemy which can be dissected for CBMs.
