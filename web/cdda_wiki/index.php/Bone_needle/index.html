“ A sharp needle made from a bone. It would be useful for making rough clothing and items ”



Notes

Interchangeable with the sewing kit.

In order to use this to repair clothing, you'll need to load it with thread or sinew. Low-level tailors will damage or destroy clothing when attempting a repair, especially when faced with badly damaged or complex clothing. Practice on your hoodie or bandana before working on that belt rig or under armor, please! Tailors of higher skill will use less thread.
