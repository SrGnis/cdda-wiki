Club Tail

Club Tail TAIL_CLUB CHIMERA
Requires: Thick Tail
Cancels: Stubby Tail, Tail Fin, Long Tail, Cattle Tail, Rodent Tail, Thick Tail, Raptor Tail, Fluffy Tail, Spiked Tail,
Point cost: 0 / Visibility: 7 / Ugliness: 2
"You have a long tail that ends in a heavy, bony club. It does not improve your balance at all, but allows for a powerful bashing attack. Prevents wearing non-fabric pants."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • This mutation removes the ability to wear gear in the left leg, right leg location.
    • Unless the gear is made of a soft material.
  • This mutation gives an additional attack, with a at minimum one in 20 chance of activating, doing 18 points of bash damage.
