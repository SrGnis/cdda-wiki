Photophore BIOLUM2 FISH INSECT ELF-A
Requires: Weak Photophore
Point cost: 2
"Your can make your photophore glow brightly."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • Can be activated, use the [ mutation menu. Costs: 1 hunger points. Per 405000 turns of use.
  • Adds encumbrance in the following locations: head : 5, if the location is covered by clothing lacking the OVERSIZE flag.
  • Some of your bodyparts now emit light: head lights up 20 squares.
