Shopping Cart

o

General

Shopping carts are vehicles that allow you to store items and pull them along separately from your inventory. They can be found inside of any store, and can be used to transport up to 150 L of items.

Currently, shopping carts have no maximum carry weight. Like any vehicle, shopping carts can be modified.

Use

Shopping carts, like other movable objects, can be grabbed by pressing 'G' and an arrow key. You can fill them by pressing 'D' and an arrow key to drop items into them, or by using the advanced inventory manager (press /) and 'D' to enter your dragged shopping cart.
