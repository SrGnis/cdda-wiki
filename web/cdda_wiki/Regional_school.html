Regional school

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search


s regional school
See Cost 5


Contains an enormous amounts of zombie children, easily over 75 in total, with a few occasional regular zombies.

School cafeteria stocks vast quantities of both perishable and non-perishable food inside their refrigerators, plus the zombie children often drop bags of candy and boxes of cookies. There is a great selection of books and skill books to be found in the school's library and in the classroom lockers, plus the playground is sure to spawn an American flag.

This location breaks the stereotype of being a safe place to fortify in a zombie apocalypse, as it is highly infested and therefore it is hard and time consuming to clear, and has too many windows that more zombies can easily enter, overall schools are best scavenged for the useful resources they have, then escaped from as soon as possible.

This is the main setting for the School scenario.
