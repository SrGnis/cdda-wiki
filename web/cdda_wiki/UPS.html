Active:

“ A unified power supply, or UPS, is a device developed jointly by military and scientific interests for use in combat and the field. The UPS is designed to power armor and some guns, but drains batteries quickly. ”



Notes

  • When used to power weapons you do not need to turn the UPS on.
  • Can be filled with batteries, but the batteries cannot be removed.
  • Can be charged using a UPS-compatible recharging station.
  • See also Advanced UPS, and Unified Power System CBM. For the more advanced UPS systems.
