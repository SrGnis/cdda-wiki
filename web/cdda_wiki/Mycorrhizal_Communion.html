Mycorrhizal Communion

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Mycorrhizal Communion TREE_COMMUNION Threshold Mutation PLANT ELF-A
Requires: Hair Roots, Roots, Rooter
Point cost: 2
"You have gained the ability to commune with trees and comprehend their ancient wisdom. Activate next to a tree to gradually gain overmap vision through connected forests."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • Can be activated, use the [ mutation menu. Costs: 0 points.
  • Must be used in a forest. There will be an initial windup time depending on what sort of roots you have. Hair Roots are slowest, Chloromorphosis is fastest.
  • Once communion has begun, you will periodically receive messages from the trees. Every few minutes, they will reveal forest tiles connected to the one you're in on your world map.
  • You will receive substantial morale bonuses from doing this, which are even higher if you have the Spiritual trait.
  • Communion will automatically end once the entire forest is revealed, and you will be unable to properly commune with this forest again. Keep moving and find more forests!
  • Plant mutants can produce mutant seeds and plant them via the Rosebuds trait. There is a special and repeatable interaction when communing with the plant which grows from this seed.
