Dreams

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
WARNING: This page may contain spoilers and ruin the joy of adventure.

Once you are sufficiently mutated, you will begin receiving dreams, relating to how strongly mutated you are toward your highest category - they are the only way a player can see their mutation category strength in-game. Once you reach tier 3 dreams, you will have a chance to breach the threshold by injecting serums.
