Notes

  • Pipe bombs have a random power between 6 and 14. This means that their basic blast radius is always a 3x3 square, and their shrapnel (if they have any) will fall within a 5x5 square.
  • Pipe bombs have a random amount of shrapnel between 0 and 4.
  • Pipe bombs have a 1 in 10 chance to do nothing when they should have exploded.
  • Pipe bombs do not light fires.
