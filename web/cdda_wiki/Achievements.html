Achievements

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Achievements, scores and kills is a tool which tracks certain statistics of current character. These have no effect on gameplay, but can be useful for self-imposed challenges, as well as letting players know how good they are doing in the game. By default it is accessed by pressing ).

Conducts

Lists self-imposed gameplay limitations. If failed, it will list the date when the conduct was failed. Conducts are a common tradition in many roguelikes, most of these conducts appear to be inspired by the fantasy roguelike Nethack.

Scores

This screen lists statistics like amount of headshots made or squares traveled.

Kills

This screen lists enemies killed by player.

Characters killed by player are listed name by name.
