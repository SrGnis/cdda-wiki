Components

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Components are items used for crafting and construction recipes (including the installation of new vehicle parts). Most of them don't have any use other than that.

You don't need to carry all the items you are crafting with, any item in a six square radius around your character can be used for crafting.

Use the Item Browser for a components and recipe search engine.
