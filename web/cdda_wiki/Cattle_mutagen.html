“ A rare substance of uncertain origins. Causes you to mutate. ”



Notes

Like all targeted mutagens, this provides the opportunity for one mutation, rather than up to three, but guarantees that the mutation will be in the targeted category.

If you already have all the relevant mutations (after your genetics-check) targeted mutagens will have no effect.

Chunks of Meat are commonplace, but the advantages available to Cattle-mutants are generally not as useful as those available to Beasts--Hooves notwithstanding.
