Triffid heart (map tile)

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
T triffid heart
See Cost 5

Finale of triffid grove dungeon. A new root wall will be created each time the heart thumps. If triffid heart is destroyed, spawn of triffids in the area will become depleted eventually.
