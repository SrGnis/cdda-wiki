Effects

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Effects, or more precisely "status effects" are a variety of states set upon a character. They vary in nature, from malicious diseases, drug effects, to special tags.

Effects, depending on their nature:

  • can be inflicted upon whole character or just a body part,
  • have a duration, but in some cases may be perpetual and removed by some specific conditions,
  • have levels of intensity (severity), which sometimes can be dependent on the effect's duration,
  • can be visible or hidden from the player (in some cases both!),
  • have their own mechanic or are informing of a mechanic in-action.
  • can be also inflicted upon NPC's or monsters (AI tags)
