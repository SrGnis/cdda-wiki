Notes

Used to control the symptoms of schizophrenia and similar ailments.

When used does the following:

  • Adds 5 points of fatigue "You feel a bit wobbly.", and every one in 50 times this is increased to 20, "You feel completely exhausted."
  • Removes Hallucinations from schizophrenia and other causes.
  • Removes High.
  • Removes the effect of itching skin, the effect of bugs crawling under your skin or other effects that make you scratch yourself compulsively. But only if it isn't caused by something really crawling under your skin.
