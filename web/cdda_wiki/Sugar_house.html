Sugar house

S sugar house
See Cost 1

A type of Eastern Canadian and (as set in the game) New England restaurant found in the wilderness where maple syrup is made and also specialises in sweet meals made from the syrup, known foods that can spawn there include fruit pancakes, maple pies, and glazed tenderloins. If you manage to find these places before these foods rot, you are one lucky survivor, even there, you may still want that sweet maple syrup for cooking these sweet recipes yourself.
