Joke monsters, can be turned off by using the 'no joke monsters'. It is highly suggested that you do so. As Thriller is a joke that ends games 10000 hp dancer zombies that can turn into hulks.

Per 0.D (Danny), these have been turned off by default, and are now part of the Category:Crazy Cataclysm Mod.
