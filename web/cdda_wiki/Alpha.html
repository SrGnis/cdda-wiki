Alpha

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Prime THRESH_ALPHA Targeted Mutation Only Not purifiable
Point cost: 1
"You're the perfect candidate to tidy this mess."

A very "safe" category. Low risk, low reward (until you breach the threshold), rather expensive to make. Promised to be the next step in human 'evolution' before the cataclysm stopped formal mutagen research.

The Medical, Alpha, Elf-A, Slime, and Troglobite mutation trees share a strong advantage over the others - none of their mutations change your anatomy in ways that prevent wearing the powerful end-game armors.

The threshold mutation of this category is called Prime ingame.
