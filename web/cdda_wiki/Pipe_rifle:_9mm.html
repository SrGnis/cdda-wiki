“ A home-made rifle. It is simply a pipe attached to a stock, with a hammer to strike the single round it holds. ”


Notes

Sometimes wielded by NPCs. Because of the 1 round capacity it is very easy to beat an NPC wielding a pipe rifle by dancing around a tree. The stupid NPCs only reload when in sight of you if their gun is empty, otherwise they walk closer. If you are a melee character hit them when they come around then move to where the tree blocks line of sight, rinse and repeat, voila, you have a dead NPC in front of you... If you are a ranged character with a gun shoot them when they come around then step to where the tree blocks line of sight, rinse and repeat. If you run out of ammo pistol whip them with the melee technique. It's nearly impossible to do this with bows and crossbows because they take a long time to fire (or reload).
