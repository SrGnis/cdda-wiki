Game Start Missions

These missions are assigned at game start. The reward for completion is a mood boost.

Background Missions

Missions assigned when you choose a character background.

Mission Background Description Requirements
Find some cosplay suits Background: Cosplay The world could have ended, but there are still some fantastic costumes out there, just waiting for you to wear them. Search them until you find them. Find and wear one of many costumes available in the world.
Dress with style Background: Modeling You need to always dress in style and fashion. Find a gown or tuxedo for your personal use. Wear a tuxedo or evening gown.
Find a tabletop game Background: Role-playing As a Game Master, it's just unacceptable that you now lack your means to play. Find any tabletop game to play with any survivors you could find, even a cards game will suffice. Obtain one of: D&D, Battlehammer, Battlehammer 40K, deck of cards, Magic cards, chess, checkers, Pictionary, Catan, Battleship, or Clue.
Find some skates Background: Skating, Roller Derby, Roller Hockey You are a skater at heart, and as such you need some skates to drive by, any kind will suffice. Wear roller skates, roller blades, or heelys.
