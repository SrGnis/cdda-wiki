Stem cell treatment

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Stem cell treatment is a disease that lightly repairs broken limbs or damaged limbs. It can be acquired by using a "Mr. Stem Cell" computer terminal, found in hospitals.

There's a 1 in 6 chance of melting your skeleton instead of mending it, damaging all the body parts.
