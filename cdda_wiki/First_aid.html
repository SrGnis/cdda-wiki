“ A full medical kit, with bandages, local anaesthetics, and rapid healing agents. Used for healing large amounts of damage. ”

Notes

  • The main way of healing other than sleeping. You can also clean bite wounds, infections and stop bleedings with this.
  • While being able to heal infections, these do not contain antibiotics, so you cannot use them in the NPC quest to get antibiotics.
  • Can be used to remove bionics, with low skill levels this can be very dangerous.
