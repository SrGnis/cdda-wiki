Weapon flags

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Check out the techniques page for extended info, Note: that page does not list all possible item flags.

  • SWEEP - Sweep Attack
  • PRECISE - Precise Strike
  • BRUTAL - Brutal Strike
  • GRAB - - unimplemented
  • WIDE - Wide Strike
  • RAPID - Rapid Strike
  • VORPAL - Vorpal Strike
  • TEC_FEINT - Feint
  • WBLOCK_1 - Block
  • WBLOCK_2 - Parry
  • WBLOCK_3 - Shield
  • TEC_COUNTER - Counterattack
  • TEC_BREAK - Grab Break
  • whip_disarm - Disarm
  • DEF_DISARM - disarm
  • STAB - Does piercing damage
  • SPEAR - Does piercing damage
  • UNARMED_WEAPON - Uses the unarmed combat skill
  • NO_UNWIELD - Can't be unwielded
  • Fuel - Uses gasoline as ammo
