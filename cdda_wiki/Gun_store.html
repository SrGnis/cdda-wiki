^>v< gun store
See Cost 5
Extras building


Lots of guns and ammo. Also some related magazines (Archery for Kids, Guns n Ammo) and gun modifications can be found here.

The ammo is always located in the right side with any SMGs. Shotguns and handguns are found in the center shelves. Rifles are place along the left side. And gun modifications and magazines are in the back left area.

All gun stores have a shooting range in the back, where you might also find other guns or ammo a client forgot there.

You may need an Improvised lockpick or a Locksmith kit to get through the front door, since the walls, windows, and doors are much tougher than normal. (you can't smash them) Alternatively, you could use a Hacksaw to saw through the barred windows. If you're all out of options, a VERY heavy weapon may allow you to break in, like using a sledge hammer on the nonmetal walls. A Jackhammer will also work to smash walls
