“ This is neither a fuel cell, nor nuclear, but the name stuck. It uses plutonium-244 as a catalyst to stabilize a complicated nanocompound that can store enormous amounts of power. Unfortunately it cannot be recharged by conventional means: expended cells had to be sent to a central reprocessing facility that almost certainly doesn't exist anymore.. ”


Notes

Plutonium cells are used to craft fusion packs, which are used as ammo for energy weaponry, some power armors, for crafting, and as power for some nuclear power plants. Advanced UPS systems take this as ammo. May also be used to make atomic items, such as the Atomic lamp. You can find these in bank safes, or from destroying and/or disassembling robotic enemies powered by these.
