High

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

A status effect given by some drugs. It decreases intellect and perception by 1.

Cannabis grants it for 60 turns (90 with the Lightweight trait). It also increases the painkiller counter by 5 (up to a maximum of 15) and hunger by 8.

Cocaine grants it for 21-[current strength] turns (+20 turns with Lightweight). It also decreases hunger by 8.

Crack grants it for 15 turns (+20 turns with Lightweight). It also increases hunger by 10.

NPCs are less trustful of high characters.

This effect can be detected by Blood Analysis and removed by Blood Filter bionics or thorazine.
