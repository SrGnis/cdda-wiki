Talk:Labrador mutt

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

"It has very low morale and it's mostly aggressive. Being a mammal, it's afraid of being hurt, fires, and the death of creatures of the same type in its proximity. It can be angered by nothing in particular, or by you hurting it. Its anger is reduced by eating meat."


Second paragraph has some grammar issues and mentions fear of fires twice. I am not an English wizard, but I re-wrote it in a way that is easy to read and has(hopefully) less errors.
