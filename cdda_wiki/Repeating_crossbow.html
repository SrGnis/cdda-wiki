“ A custom made mechanical large crossbow, with a wooden magazine that holds 10 bolts. Single bolt reload. Bolts fired from this weapon have a good chance of remaining intact for re-use. ”


Notes

  • Based on the real life chinese crossbow, the personal protection variant, not the heavy variant. As such it has little power and doesn't do much damage against most zombies, and is useless against anything with armor unless explosive bolts are used.
