A place in the wilderness where those who perished in the Cataclysm were buried, only for many of them to reanimate as they were being buried, expect a lot of zombies here, and corpse filled pits of the more fortunate who didn't happen to revive... Is surrounded by a barbed wire fence, but that barely fazes the plentiful walking corpses...
