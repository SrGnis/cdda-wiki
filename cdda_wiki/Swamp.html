Swamp

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search


F swamp
See Cost 4
Extras field

A forested area dotted with pools of water.

Spawns swamp creatures. Except during winter. Watch out for sinkholes.

Some of the pools of water contain salt water instead of regular water, which is a reliable way to collect salt for meat jerky. Some pools are also deep water instead of shallow water, allowing you to train swimming.
