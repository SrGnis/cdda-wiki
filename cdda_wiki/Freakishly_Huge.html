Freakishly Huge

Jump to navigation Jump to search
Freakishly Huge HUGE URSINE CATTLE
Requires: Inconveniently Large, Large
Additional requirement: Extremely Strong, Insanely Strong
Cancels: Little, Tiny
Changes to: Huge
Point cost: -2 / Visibility: 4 / Ugliness: 4
"You have grown even more massive, to the point where you cannot usefully fit into human-sized clothing or vehicles. Much of it is powerful muscle mass (Strength +4), but it's a real pain to haul around."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • This mutation removes the ability to wear gear in the torso, left leg, right leg, left arm, right arm, left hand, right hand, head, left foot, right foot locations.
    • Any invalid gear that is equipped in the slots above will be destroyed on mutation.
  • Reduces maximum Hit points of each body part by 6.
  • Increases fatigue increase over time by 15.0%.
  • Increases maximum carry weight by 10.0%.
  • Modifies strength by 4.
  • Makes it harder to get into vehicles, which can cause Pain.
( Edit Notes )
