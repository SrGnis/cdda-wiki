Card reader

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
6 card reader
Movement 0
Action cardreader
Flags noitem

This card reader console is found in front of science labs and military bunkers. If you have a science ID card or military ID card, respectively, you can open the doors by examining it. This consumes the ID card.

You could attempt to hack it with an electrohack. The chance to succeed at this depends on your computer skill.

A nearby EMP grenade blast might destroy it or force it to open.
