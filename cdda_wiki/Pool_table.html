# pool table
Movement 200
Action none
Flags transparent flammable collapses deconstruct indoors

Pool tables can be found in mansions, often accompanied by a number of pool cues and pool balls.

Construction

Deconstructs Into:

  • Ingredients: 4 two by four, 4 rag, 6-10 nails

Smashes Into:

  • Ingredients: 2-6 two by four, 4-12 nails, 1 splintered wood
