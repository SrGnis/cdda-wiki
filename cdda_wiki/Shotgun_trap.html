“ A tool. ”



“ A simple tripwire is attached to the trigger of a loaded sawn-off shotgun. When pulled, the shotgun fires. Two rounds are used; the first time the trigger is pulled, one or two may be used. ”

Notes

A very dangerous trap, both to you and zombies. Very effective when you set them up, but beware that you aren't the only one with the idea. Survivor houses can contain these, and surprise buckshot to the chest is not very fun. Watch your step!
