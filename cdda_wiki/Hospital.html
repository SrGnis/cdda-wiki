H hospital
See Cost 5
Extras building

Always occupies 3x3 square. Hospitals are a good place to find all kinds of drugs and healing items-maybe you'll even be able to fix that broken limb.

It is also the starting location for the Challenge-Abandoned scenario. Lots of fun! for the zombies It's also a place plagued with zombies. LOTS of them. To be specific, mostly spitter zombies and boomers, isn't as badly infested as a megastore, but is still a medium to high risk area.

Always has a blood test lab as center square, as well as "Mr. Stem Cell" computer terminal somewhere, an experimental limb-regeneration machine.
