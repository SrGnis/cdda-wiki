“ A translucent hexagonal chunk of wax, filled with dense, milky, bitter and acidic-tasting jelly. Though some hold it as a panacea, it doesn't have any medical benefit. Still it is rich with the most beneficial substances the hive can produce. ”



Notes

This substance is baby bee food created in the head of a nurse bee.

All larva bees are fed royal jelly for three days and the diet then changes according to what the workers decide the bee should be. (Royal jelly for queens, a 1-1 mix of pollen and honey for workers, and a 3 pollen 1 honey mix for drones.)

This item used to be a cure-all for many diseases, but as of 0.E (Ellison), that use was removed from this item and replaced with a rare drug called Panaceus.

While royal jelly is now pretty useless by itself now, it can still be used to cook a very healthy and filling recipe called royal beef, which increases the player's health stat by 10.
