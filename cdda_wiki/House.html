The suburban house, a staple of looting. Houses always have the following four room types. As of 0.A (Kaufman), there are more than one varieties of houses that can be generated under the house map tile.

House Varieties

  • Original Four Room (detailed below)
  • Vacant/Boarded-up House
  • Modern House
  • Hippie House (Contains Marijuana and joints, has glass walls in many places)
  • Liquor Hippie House (Has a pond out back, rock walls. Contains a wide variety of alcohol)
  • If the house has a red carpet in the tiles version, beware! the place is trapped, booby trapped doors, landmines, the works
  • Wasp's nests: these houses are distinguished by their paper walls, and house a large number of giant wasps, they often contain high-tech loot.
  • Spider nests: much like wasp's nest, but house giant black widows and are full of webs.

Rooms

Living room

The area just behind the front door. Contains book cases and a desk. You'll usually find assorted clothing and other miscellaneous items, such as lighters and magazines.

Kitchen

It contains a fridge, sink, oven, cupboards, and maybe a table.

Items

  • salt water
  • bleach
  • ammonia
  • broom
  • mop
  • rubber gloves
  • dust mask
  • plastic bottle
  • sewing kit
  • thread
  • rag
  • scissors
  • Long string

Bedroom

A big room with a bed and a dresser. You'll usually find miscellaneous clothing and other items in the dresser. Lying around you can find other useful items such as sewing kits, containers, sunglasses, etc. Occasionally there's two bedrooms.

Bathroom

There's always a functioning toilet here that you can use as a source of limited unclean water. You can also find all kind of meds here, including aspirin, multivitamin, Cough syrup, Non-drowsy cough syrup, bandages, first aid kit, and occasionally rarer drugs.

Other Rooms

Among the four basic rooms in whatever shape or form they may take, there are other rooms that can potentially be generated with a house:

  • Basement
  • Garage

Changes

  • 0.7 (Lindqvist) - House generation fixes.
  • 0.A (Kaufman) - Added new house varieties and rooms.
