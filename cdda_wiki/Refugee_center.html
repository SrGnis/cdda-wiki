+ Refugee center
See Cost 1

The Refugee center is a location, it is generated on the end of a road, and is surrounded by roads, if after reading a road map you see a 3x3 square surrounded by roads you are likely to find the center there.Verify (The current view cost value isn't set correctly)

This location is around 3x3 in size, will contain beds, sheets, pillows, and may spawn tinned food, some misc tools. The guards may or may not have guns. And refugees might have tools, beggars don't hold any items.

How to find one

From the Evac Center's computer you can find directions to one of the Refugee Centers the nearest one to the Evac Center. Here there will be various NPC's Beggars, Refugees, Guards, a Broker, Arsonist, Representative, Merchant, Doctor, and later after some Representative's missions a Hunter.

Missions

The Merchant will give a mission to kill Zombies which will spawn a shocker zombie when taken the pack of 12-20 zombies can be killed before hand, Bandits, and to establish a connection with the Ranch, and to get energy cells for the Center.

The Broker will buy some long lasting flour, and such food.

A guard can give a mission to gather and bring him 100 cigarettes.

The doctor will give some quests.

Beggars

5 people in the lobby are beggars who aren't allowed in due to their uselessness. Most of them can be convinced to become followers, but they have awful stats and traits (pacifist, bad knees, glass jaw, etc..) and aren't fit to do anything except menial tasks.

  • Reena Sandhu, teacher, is rightfully scared of outside world. Convincing her is a roll against 100 difficulty, which can be made easier by repeatedly giving her food.
  • Dino Dave, schizophrenic living in cardboard fort. Finishing cardboard castle will require 40 small cardboard boxes, 200 duct tape, 10 large plastic sheets, 10 cardboard boxes and convincing the merchant to let him stay somewhere where he will not disturb other refugees. When castle is finished he will live on rooftop of refugee center.
  • Luo Meizhen, mushroom scientist. Luo can be recruited if you don't insult her, give her an `alien fungus chunk` and convince most of other beggars to leave.
  • Brandon Garder, sickly beggar. He will agree to leave only if Dino Dave is sorted out (which means building his castle).
  • Yusuke Taylor, mutant, the only beggar with average stats. He will leave if other beggars except Dave will agree to leave.

Visitors

Arsonist will exchange 2 bags of commercial fertilizer for 10 molotov cocktails, and will sell explosives.

Mercenary can be recruited by giving him 750 merch. He has guaranteed Tough and Psychopath traits.

The Representative will give missions to kill bandits, a worm, in the mission to find a mole a new guard will spawn who can be chatted with over and over until provoked killing the provoked renegade guard will finish the mission correctly. After killing some bandits for the Representative the Hunter should spawn who acts as a Merchant who sells some food items, and has 1000 cash. Completing Representative's missions will also point towards Exodii base.

Basement

There are stairs leading down in refugee center, and there are mentions of people living there in some NPC dialogue. However, currently it isn't accessible.
