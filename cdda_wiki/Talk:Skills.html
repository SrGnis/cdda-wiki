Focus

The previous mention of a skill not getting practiced unless the focus level was above the skill level was replaced with an explanation that the amount a skill is practiced is multiplied by focus/100. Looking through the source code I couldn't find any check that directly or indirectly compared the current skill level with the focus when a skill was being practiced. If somebody finds that somewhere, please amend my change. Previously, though, a search for "focus" on the wiki shows lots of information on how focus is increased or decreased, but none that actually described what focus is used for (except for when it directly increases or decreases your stats) and a mention on the Morale page suggesting you "spend" your focus when your morale and focus pool are high. --Waylandsmith (talk) 23:10, 25 December 2015 (UTC)
