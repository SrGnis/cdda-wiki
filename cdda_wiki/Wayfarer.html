Wayfarer WAYFARER Possible Starting Trait Targeted Mutation Only
Point cost: -8
"Whether from personal choice or childhood trauma, traveling with vehicles is off-limits to you, even if your life depended on it."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • Moving through the New England Wasteland with good items will get a whole lot harder with this trait, as vehicles allow for heavier artillery to be moved to new homes.
  • If you want to move home with more stuff, you may need a duffel bag as well as high strength traits or strength increasing bionics and/or mutations. Even then, moving on foot will take a VERY long time before you find your next home...
  • This prevents you from taking control of a vehicle (even via a remote control). Despite the description appearing to say otherwise, it does not prohibit you from riding in a vehicle.
