Mycus Sporogenesis M_FERTILE Targeted Mutation Only Threshold Mutation MYCUS
Requires: Mycus Spores
Cancels: Mycus Blossoms, Mycus Bloom
Changes to: Mycus Provenance
Point cost: 6 / Visibility: 5 / Ugliness: 3
"Local organisms seem to regard open spore-dispersal as a danger, and attack the Mycus. Accordingly our local spore sacs remain quiescent, until local guidance determines that it is safe to germinate. Rapid development taxes local nutrient reserves but ensures swift and effective growth."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • Can be activated, use the [ mutation menu. Costs: 8 hunger, thirst points.
  • Creates Spore clouds on activation, costs 8 hunger and thirst.
