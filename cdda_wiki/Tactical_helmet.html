“ A lightweight black helmet that provides excellent protection from all sorts of damage. ”

This is a somewhat heavy clothing item made of kevlar and plastic, which covers 90% of your head, and which has no storage capacity. It is bulky, encumbers you a bit, and will provide some warmth to the areas it covers.

It offers low protection against bashing attacks and low protection against cutting ones (that includes bullets), while offering no protection from environmental hazards.

It reduces the morale hit when getting wet (and can even transform it into morale bonus), provides a weak block when wielded as a weapon.

This clothing item can be repaired, fitted (if appropriate) and reinforced via the mechanics skill, by using a soldering iron and kevlar plates.
