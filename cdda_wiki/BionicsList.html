Adrenaline Pump 
bio_adrenaline
A stimulator system has been surgically implanted alongside your adrenal glands, allowing you to trigger your bodys adrenaline response at the cost of some bionic power. torso by 6
Active Defense System 
bio_ads
A thin forcefield surrounds your body, continually draining power. Anything attempting to penetrate this field has a chance of being deflected at the cost of energy, reducing their ability to deal damage. Bullets will be deflected more than melee weapons and those in turn more than massive objects. torso by 10, head by 1, left arm by 1, right arm by 1, left leg by 2, right leg by 2
Alarm System 
bio_alarm
A motion-detecting alarm system will notice almost all movement within a fifteen-foot radius, and will silently alert you. This is very useful during sleep, or if you suspect a cloaked pursuer. torso by 2, head by 1
Squeaky Ankles 
bio_ankles
In a cruel twist of fate, a poorly executed CBM surgery has given you a pair of useless bionics which make squeaking noises when you walk. left leg by 3, right leg by 3
Alloy Plating - Arms 
bio_armor_arms
The flesh on your arms has been surgically replaced by alloy plating. Provides passive protection and can be used in conjunction with bionic martial arts. left arm by 4, right arm by 4
Protective Lenses 
bio_armor_eyes
Your eye sockets have been surgically sealed with highly protective mirrored lenses and your tear ducts have been re-routed to your mouth. When you cry, you must spit out or swallow your tears. eyes by 1

bio_armor_head
The flesh on your head has been surgically replaced by alloy plating, protecting both your head and jaw regions. head by 5, mouth by 1
Alloy Plating - Legs 
bio_armor_legs
The flesh on your legs has been surgically replaced by alloy plating. Provides passive protection and can be used in conjunction with bionic martial arts. left leg by 6, right leg by 6

bio_armor_torso
The flesh on your torso has been surgically replaced by alloy plating, protecting it from physical trauma. torso by 10

You have a battery draining attachment, and thus can make use of the energy contained in normal, everyday batteries. Use E to drain charge from a battery. Can be toggled on and off at will. torso by 25

bio_blade
A deadly foot-long blade made of advanced material now resides inside your forearm, capable of being extended through the back of your wrist at the cost of a small amount of power. Though exceptionally sharp, it will prevent you from holding anything else while extended. right arm by 5, right hand by 2
Shotgun Arm 
bio_shotgun
Concealed in your left arm is a single shot 12 gauge shotgun. Activate the bionic to fire and reload the shotgun. left arm by 15
Optical Dampers 
bio_blindfold
The lenses surgically installed over your eyes can be tinted to block out incoming light. non
Blood Analysis 
bio_blood_anal
Small sensors have been surgically implanted in your heart, allowing you to analyze your blood. This will detect many illnesses, drugs, and other conditions. torso by 5
Blood Filter 
bio_blood_filter
A filtration system in your heart allows you to actively filter out chemical impurities, primarily drugs. It will have limited impact on viruses. Note that it is not a targeted filter; ALL drugs in your system will be affected. torso by 10
Cable Charger System 
bio_cable
You have a complex port surgically mounted above your hip. While active, it will recharge bionic power when connected to a power source via jumper cable. torso by 10
Subdermal Carbon Filament 
bio_carbon
Lying just beneath your skin is a thin armor made of carbon nanotubes. This reduces bashing damage by 2 and cutting damage by 4. torso by 5, head by 2, left arm by 2, right arm by 2, left hand by 1, right hand by 1, left leg by 3, right leg by 3, left foot by 1, right foot by 1
Chain Lightning 
bio_chain_lightning
Your body is equipped with a chain lightning generator, allowing you to emit a blast of lightning at a target, leaving a trail of lightning in its wake, jumping to additional targets within 4 tiles of the previous target. torso by 16, left arm by 4, right arm by 4
Bionic Claws 
bio_claws
Vicious claws have been surgically installed inside your fingers, allowing you to extend and retract them at the cost of a small amount of power. These do considerable cutting damage, but prevent you from holding anything else while extended. left hand by 5, right hand by 5
Internal Climate Control 
bio_climate
Throughout your body lies a network of thermal piping, which eases the effects of high and low ambient temperatures when activated. torso by 8, head by 2, left arm by 2, right arm by 2, left hand by 1, right hand by 1, left leg by 3, right leg by 3, left foot by 1, right foot by 1

bio_cloak
This high-power system uses a set of cameras and LEDs to make you blend into your background, rendering you fully invisible to normal vision. However, you may be detected by infrared, sonar, etc. torso by 15, head by 2, left arm by 3, right arm by 3, left hand by 1, right hand by 1, left leg by 4, right leg by 4, left foot by 1, right foot by 1
Close Quarters Battle 
bio_cqb
Bionic processors and databanks, loaded with martial arts combat programs, are surgically integrated into your nervous system. Whilst active, the CQB module will improve your hand-to-hand combat skills, but prevents you from improving them through combat experience. head by 4
Bionic-Induced Deformity 
bio_deformity
A combination of poor surgical installation and unpleasant scarring has lead to the malfunctioning bionic catastrophe you call your face. People who mind that you look like a dime-store Frankenstein will react poorly to your appearance. head by 4, mouth by 1
Wired Reflexes 
bio_dex_enhancer
Your reaction time has been greatly enhanced with bionic nerve stimulators, giving you a +2 bonus to dexterity. torso by 6, left arm by 4, right arm by 4, left leg by 8, right leg by 8
Expanded Digestive System 
bio_digestion
You have been outfitted with three synthetic stomachs and industrial-grade intestines. Not only can you extract much more nutrition from food, but you are highly resistant to foodborne illness, and can sometimes eat rotten food. torso by 20

Ethanol Burner 
bio_ethanol
You burn alcohol as fuel in an extremely efficient reaction. torso by 26

Diamond Cornea 
bio_eye_enhancer

Telescopic Eyes 
bio_eye_optic

bio_glowy

bio_geiger
