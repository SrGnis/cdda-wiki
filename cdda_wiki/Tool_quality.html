Tools have a quality of a certain level, indicating what the tool does and how well it does it. They are used for crafting and other tasks. For example, an aluminum bat can be used as a crude hammer and thus has a HAMMER 1 stat. But it is no where near a useful for hammering as an actual hammer, which has a HAMMER 3 stat.

The item browser has lists of tool qualities, and the items which have those qualities. For the experimental version, and stable.

analysis

Level Range: 1

Used in Chemical crafting when working with Mutagens.

anesthesia

Level Range: 1

Used with specialized medical equipment for surgery.

anvil

Level Range: 1 - 3

Used in Metalwork crafting.

awl

Level Range: 1 - 2

Used in Leatherworking.

bolt turning

Level Range: 1 - 2 (3 with Magiclysm)

Level 1+ is used for basic mechanic tasks and crafting.

Level 2+ is used for intermediate and advanced mechanic tasks and crafting.

butchering

Level Range: -120 - 37 (45 with Magiclysm)

Level of butchering affects the end results when butchering corpses. (Recommended not to pulp bodies you intend to butcher or dissect).

bullet pulling

Level Range: 2

Used for disassembling firearm ammunition.

chiseling

Level Range: 1 - 3

Used in Woodworking and Metalworking crafting.

chromatography

Level Range: Unknown

Used in both analysis and purification.

clamping

Level Range: 1

Used to craft a Workshop Toolbox, and build an Arc Furnace.

containing

Level Range: 1

Used for holding items, and for Food and Chemical crafting.

concentration

Level Range: 1

Used for Mutagen crafting.

curved needle

Level Range: 1 (2 with Magiclysm)

Used in Tailoring, especially when working with neoprene.

cutting

Level Range: 1 (5 with Magiclysm)

Can be used to cut up an item, or write up on an item.

Also used in crafting.

churn

Level Range: 1 - 2

Used in churning milk to make butter.

digging

Level Range: 1 - 3

Level 1+ Upturn earth, Dig pit here, Fill pit / Tamp ground, Dig water channel here.

Level 2+ Clear rubble.

distilling

Level Range: 1 - 2

Used in the distillation of alcohol and chemicals.

drilling

Level Range: 1 - 3

Used in crafting recipes.

filing

Level Range: 1 - 2

Used in crafting recipes.

fine bolt turning

Level Range: 1 (4 with Magiclysm)

Used in crafting recipes.

fine cutting

Level Range: 1 - 5

Used in dissection of Corpses. (Recommended that you don't pulp corpses you intend to dissect).

fine distillation

Level Range: 1

Used in the distillation when working with chemicals and mutagens.

fine hammering

Level Range: 1 (2 with Magiclysm)

Used in crafting recipes, usually in the crafting of weapon mods.

prying

This quality is used for some constructions and recipes. However it has a secondary function: it can be used pry open objects.

Prying 1 and above can open sealed crates, and lift manhole covers.

Prying 2 and above can open locked wooden doors and windows. (Both can trigger alarms, while windows have a chance of breaking).
