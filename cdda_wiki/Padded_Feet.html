Padded Feet

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Padded Feet PADDED_FEET BEAST FELINE LUPINE URSINE
Cancels: Toe Talons, Hooves, Roots, Rooter, Chloromorphosis, Leg Tentacles
Point cost: 1 / Visibility: 1
"The bottoms of your feet are strongly padded. You receive no movement penalty for not wearing shoes, and even receive a 10% bonus when moving barefoot."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • Unlike the traits that compete with it, this one still permits wearing footwear. Depending on the temperature, you might have to decide whether the speed boost is worth risking frostbite.
