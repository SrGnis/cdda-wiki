“ A tool. ”



“ A spring-loaded pair of steel jaws. Use it to set it on the ground, creating a trap that will ensnare and damage anything that steps on it. If you are carrying a shovel, you will have the option of burying it. ”

Notes

  • A bear trap deals 35 HP of damage, though tiny animals can walk through it unharmed. More one time damage than something like a nail board trap, but beware you're going to have to reset it to use it again.
  • Can be buried if you have a shovel.
