Comestibles/Seeds

From The Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Seeds can be planted to grow various fruits, vegetables, mushrooms and otherworldly horrors, why would you do that? You traitor to mother earth!. Just place them in a dirt mound and wait.

For a more detailed description of how farming works, see the farming guide.


  • Thirst and hunger go up by 1 every 5 minutes (50 turns); so a quench of 12 lasts for 1 hour (600 turns). This can be changed by Bionics such as the Recycler Unit, or mutations such as the Light Eater trait.
  • Quench MAY be less than zero — salt water and liquor make you thirstier.
  • For rot measurements (spoils), it is hours after the item's creation. For most other things (including painkillers), it is measured in turns; 10 turns = 1 minute.
  • Actual spoilage time depends on ambient temperature, stopping completely in Freezing temperature.
  • Crafted food is created roughly as fresh as the average of all perishable ingredients used (the remaining freshness is rescaled to new spoil time).
  • One stimulation point lasts for 5 minutes, also comestibles with caff effect decrease fatigue for 3 points per one stimulation point.
  • Fun can also depend on traits and mutations. A cannibal eating human flesh gets a lot more fun out of eating.
  • Most drinks come in containers, and cannot be drunk off the floor.
  • Most meds are capable of dissolving in water, if not held in a watertight container.
  • Most meds have 0 volume and weight.
  • Prices might differ while trading. If a second value is included in the price, this is the price a NPC trader uses.
  • Weight is per unit, so if you find a certain Comestible, it weighs the weight multiplied by the amount of charges plus the weight of any container.


See Also

  • Comestible JSON Info
  • Seeds that have a red colored fruit field will not provide seeds on growing, if it is green they will produce seeds.
  • Growth period is in days; it is modified by the season length setting in-game, and it is based on real world season length (91 days). The default in-game season length is 14 days. So if you play on default season lengths, the grow period takes one-third of the time listed here.Verify (The in-game season length default is actually 91 days. This doesn't make much sense.)
