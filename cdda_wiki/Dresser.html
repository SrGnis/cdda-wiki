Dressers are found abundantly in houses. They often contain assorted articles of clothing.

Construction

Made From:

  • Tool: wood saw, (hammer, stone hammer, hatchet, nail gun)
  • Ingredients: 6x two by four, 8x nails

Deconstructs Into:

  • Ingredients: 6 two by four, 6-8 nails

Smashes Into:

  • Ingredients: 2-6 two by four, 4-12 nails, 1 splintered wood

Notes:

Holds 8000 volume.
