0.F (Frank) is a version of Cataclysm that was released on July 3, 2021. Three bugfix releases 0.F-3 (Frank-3), 0.F-2 (Frank-2) and 0.F-1 (Frank-1) were also released.


For more information about this release see the 0.F (Frank) page.
