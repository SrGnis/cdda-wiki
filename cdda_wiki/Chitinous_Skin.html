Chitinous Skin CHITIN SPIDER
Requires: Rough Skin
Cancels: Rough Skin, Scales, Thick Scales, Sleek Scales, Feathers, Down, Lightly Furred, Furry, Shaggy Fur, Gray Fur, Sleek Fur, Lynx Fur, Chitinous Armor, Chitinous Plate, Hairy Chitin, Furred Chitin, Furred Plate, Phelloderm, Bark
Changes to: Chitinous Armor, Hairy Chitin
Point cost: 2 / Visibility: 3 / Ugliness: 2
"Your epidermis has turned into a thin, flexible layer of chitin. It provides minor protection from cutting wounds. Slightly reduces wet effects."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • Gives wet protection:
    • head 1 ignored protection.
    • left leg 5 ignored protection.
    • right leg 5 ignored protection.
    • left foot 1 ignored protection.
    • right foot 1 ignored protection.
    • left arm 4 ignored protection.
    • right arm 4 ignored protection.
    • left hand 1 ignored protection.
    • right hand 1 ignored protection.
    • torso 10 ignored protection.
  • Provides the following armor values.
    • All locations: 2 cut protection, 2 bash protection.
