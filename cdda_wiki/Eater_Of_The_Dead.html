Eater Of The Dead EATDEAD Targeted Mutation Only Threshold Mutation CHIMERA RAT MOUSE
Requires: Intestinal Fortitude
Point cost: 4
"Eating rotting, long-dead flesh is good for the struggle, and safe--if still completely unappealing--for you."

(View - Edit Notes )Notes

  • This replaces Intestinal Fortitude, and includes the benefits of Intestinal Fortitude. You can also drink water without needing to create clean water.
